This is a free online Malayalam proficiency test.
This online level test will give you an approximate indication of your Malayalam language level

  • There will be a total of 20 Multiple choice questions
  • You can either choose an option or leave it and press the submit button to move to the next question.
  • At the end of the test, your score will be displayed along with your Malayalam Language level (ML).

Note that ML5(Advanced) and ML6(Proficient) levels are not available in this test
