Nira Mahajani

❮ ❯

Glyph Set
The solid design of Nira Mahajani Font provides visual contrast and readability, creating an eye-catching look that stands out in any project.The unorderly nature of the letters is suitable for both modern and classic designs, giving the typeface versatility and broad appeal. Nira is a superfamily of fonts designed to bring less-common Indian scripts into the mainstream. The Malayalam word 'Nira' means "row,""dynasty," etc. The family is unicode compatable and is licensed under the IDCL Free Font Licence (IFFL). Nira Mahajani comes in four styles, Nira Mahajani Bold, Nira Mahajani Bold Condensed, Nira Mahajani Regular, and Nira Mahajani Regular Oblique. Each of these styles has its own unique characteristics and glyphs, making them ideal for a range of applications.
