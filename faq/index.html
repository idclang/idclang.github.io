Preserve and Promote

1. Publishing an article in IDCL

Publishing an article in IDCL requires contacting us first. You may contact us through our website or through our social media accounts. Articles may be sent to us in text (TXT), rich text (DOCX, DOC, ODF), PDF*, and markdown (MD) formats. Articles will be published after reviewing. You may send articles in English, Telugu, Malayalam, Kannada, Tamil, Tulu**, Gondi, Bengali in their own writing system, and in other languages that uses a script supported by Unicode.

* You may send a PDF file only if your article is in Latin script and is digitally formatted.

2. Requesting Documentation

If you happen to be the native speaker of an under-resourced language, or have a clear knowledge about such, you may request for a documentation.

Points to consider before requesting documentation

  1. Details about phonology
  2. Orthography, if exists
  3. Information about literature (if any), oral traditions or other artforms

3. Proposing font for an under-resourced language’s script.

We create both Unicode and non-Unicode fonts for under-resourced languages based on a priority list. IDCL lists out the scripts according to our criteria, and then add them as projects. You may propose a font for the script of an under-resourced language. Unicode’s compatibility is not necessary for such. However, scripts constructed by the person who is proposing for such is considered as a custom font, which will not be created free of cost. It is to be noted that your proposal is subject of evaluation by the Idclang team.

 

4. Responsibility of Contributors

We do not display or share the information about our contributors, unless we are authorised to do so.

5. Gathering of Personal Information

We collect email address from those who establish contact with us through our contact page, and such information shall not be shared with anyone else. 

6. Protection of Personal- Information

Almost all the resources provided by us require no verification of sign in from users to download or use.
