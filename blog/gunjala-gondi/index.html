Gondi Language

Gondi is a Dravidian language spoken in the Indian states of Madhya Pradesh, Telangana, Maharashtra, and Chhattisgarh. It belongs to the South Central Dravidian branch of the Dravidian language family and classified as an endangered language. It has over 2.98 million autochthonous speakers, but the language, and it’s dialects, are diminishing. Gondi is a dialect-centric language, sometimes they are pertained as Gondi languages.
