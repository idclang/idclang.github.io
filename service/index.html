Developing digital tools

With the lack of proper digital tools, people tend to use a major language as their medium of communication through digital platforms. Sometimes romanisation of scripts becomes a problem by discouraging people from using their own writing system. To tackle problems like these, we commence projects to develop the right tools needed for encouraging people to use their mother tongue on the digital platform.

What we do

Documentation

We gather information from native speakers about the nuances of their language.

Building tools

We develop digital tools to help native speakers and language learners

Fonts

We develop fonts for various Indian languages, it doesn’t matter whether the script has Unicode compatibility or not. You can also order custom fonts.

Resources

Our main focus is on collecting as much resource as we can. We publish digital books, articles, and other source materials with the help of interested people.

Benefits

If you would like to help under-resourced languages, then you can order custom fonts, artworks, poster designs, e-book templates and more at fair commissions.
