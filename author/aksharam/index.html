Aksharam

A Journey of Letters

Explore the nuances of South-East Asian scripts through ravishing infographic posts. Aksharam covers various topics such as Telugu language, Brahmic scripts, Classical literature and more. You will find  fascinating facts about various Indic writing systems, their evolution, and current status.

Follow Instagram

Behind the name

Aksharam comes from Sanskrit, and it means letter in various South Indian languages.

The Misson

The objective of this account is to educate people on writing systems, particularly Indian scripts, and their evolution. By creating visual elements that convey the message more effectively than words, Aksharam aims to bring knowledge to everyone.
